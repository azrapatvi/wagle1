Wagle Enterprises is a dedicated Fly Ash Brick Manufacturing Unit located at Ratnagiri Mirzole, M.I.D.C Zone 11. We specialize in producing high-quality bricks that combine industrial strength with environmental responsibility.

Our manufacturing process utilizes fly ash — a byproduct of thermal power plants — transforming industrial waste into construction-grade bricks. This innovative approach reduces landfill burden while delivering superior building materials.

Every brick we produce carries the promise of durability, precision, and sustainability — the cornerstones of modern responsible construction.

Core Strengths

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Eco-Friendly

Made from fly ash — industrial waste repurposed into premium bricks. Our process produces significantly lower CO₂ emissions compared to traditional clay bricks, contributing to a cleaner environment.

Strong & Durable

Our fly ash bricks deliver exceptional compressive strength, excellent thermal insulation, and outstanding resistance to moisture and weathering — outperforming conventional bricks in long-term structural integrity.

Smart Solutions

Precision-engineered brick dimensions ensure faster masonry, reduced mortar consumption, and a flawless finish. Our standardized sizing integrates seamlessly with modern construction workflows.

Wagle Enterprises fly ash bricks are manufactured under strict quality control, meeting IS 12894 standards. Each batch is tested for compressive strength, dimensional accuracy, and water absorption.

Suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial construction, our bricks are preferred by builders and contractors across the Ratnagiri region.
